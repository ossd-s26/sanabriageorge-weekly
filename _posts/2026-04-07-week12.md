## Industry Use and Participation in Open Source 
After the class discussion, I began to see open source in a new way. I believed it was just simply "free software" but it is more deeply connected to how tech industry operates. It is clear that these large companies rely on and contribute to open source porjects as it benefits them. What is interesting is how these companies contribute with open source in order to save time and resources. This creates a foundation with which everyone can build upon it faster. What is also fascinating is how industries, help fund open source projects. Without financial support these projects will struggle in improving. But the question of how are these open source projects being influenced arise.
