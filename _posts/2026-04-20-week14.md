## Final Stretch
This week we began working on our presentation due to the fact that all of us in the team has at least one PR merged. So during class time instead of continuing making PRs or finding new issues we promoted to creating  slides to present our contributions. This went smoothly as we all split the slide duties and input information regarding what we each individually did. Afterwards, the next day we had our weekly meeting and in this meeting we split up the slides so that everyone has a chance to speak. Then we did a practice presentation in order to see how long it takes for us and thankfully we made the 20 minute mark which was a concern of mine. I am looking forward to sharing what I learned and what I contributed to the whole class next week. I am proud of the work me and my teammates have done!
